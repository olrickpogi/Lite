To begin with, Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng San Pablo (PLSP) is a public institution dedicated to fostering quality education and holistic development for its students. Founded to serve the community of San Pablo and neighboring areas, PLSP aims to shape competent, socially responsible, and globally competitive individuals. Additionally, through its programs, PLSP continues to inspire and empower students to lead, innovate, and make a meaningful impact in society. In short, Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng San Pablo embodies a philosophy that values education as a transformative force, with a mission focused on accessibility and community service, all while aspiring towards excellence in various educational endeavors.




Philosophy


The school is deeply committed towards the integral formation of the human person, with a profound faith in God, in his fellow men and himself by providing its students the full development of their physical, intellectual, social and cultural endowment for effective participation in various professions and industrial occupations and to enable them to enjoy reasonable quality of life to be able to contribute to the upliftment of the human society.


Vision


Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng San Pablo envisions itself as a Center of Excellence in 2035, upholding glocally-responsive education dedicated to transform lives and empower communities.


Mission


The Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng San Pablo mission statement is to create a learning community that generates God-loving and holistic graduates prepared for civic engagement and academic and life success by strengthening and synergizing instruction, research and extension, administration and student development.


Goals


1. University and Academic Excellence

2. Sustainable Linkages and Community Engagement Programs

3. Relevant and Responsive Governance

4. Strengthened University Research Culture

5. Nurturing and Impactful Student Life and Success Program


Objectives


1. To provide relevant knowledge and to develop the skills, competencies, habits, attitudes and values of the students that are required in the chosen profession.

2. To advance human knowledge through study and research.

3. To give professional training in specific, cultural, technological, industrial and vocational fields.

4. To mold students to become professionally competent, creative, and capable of making free, morally upright choices and decisions and serve as positive agents of change in their respective community.

5. To provide opportunities for personal growth in leadership and community building through a network of student curricular, extra and co-curricular activities as well as human resource development programs for continuous growth of students, faculty and staff.

College of Arts and Sciences (CAS) Programs - Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng San Pablo

Bachelor of Arts in Political Science

This course/program explores the foundations of governance and politics, public policy, and political theory etc. Moreover, this program prepares students for careers in public service, law, international relations, and political analysis, emphasizing critical thinking and social responsibility.

Bachelor of Science in Psychology

Delve into the science of human behavior and mental processes. The Psychology program combines theory and research to prepare students for careers in counseling, human resources, and further studies in clinical or industrial psychology.

Bachelor of Arts in Communication

Master the art of communication through media studies, journalism, and public relations. This program equips students with skills for careers in broadcasting, digital media, advertising, and corporate communications.

Bachelor of Science in Public Administration

Gain the knowledge and skills essential for effective governance and public service. The BS in Public Administration program at PLSP prepares students for careers in government agencies, non-profit organizations, and public sector management. With a curriculum focused on policy analysis, public finance, organizational management, and community development, this program develops future leaders equipped to serve society with integrity and accountability.
